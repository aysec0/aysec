Refund Policy

If a course doesn't deliver, I refund. No interrogation, no "show me what you tried".

Single-course purchase

30-day, no-questions-asked refund. Email me within 30 days of purchase with your order number, and you get a full refund to your original payment method within 5 business days.

You can keep using what you've already accessed during that 30 days — I'm not going to gate refund eligibility on "you watched too much".

All-Access (lifetime bundle)

30-day refund on the same terms as single courses. After 30 days, no refund — but you also have permanent access to everything, so the math should work out.

Pro Monthly subscription

Cancel any time from your dashboard or by emailing me. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period — no pro-rata refunds for partial months.

Mistakenly auto-renewed and didn't use it that month? Email me, I'll refund it.

How to request a refund

One email with your order number is all I need. I usually process within 24 hours; payment provider may take 3-5 business days to reflect the refund on your card.

Edge cases

  • Chargebacks — please email me first. Chargebacks cost me a fee and you'd get the same outcome faster by just asking.
  • Currency conversions — refund matches the original charge in original currency. Conversion losses are out of my hands.
  • Team licenses — refund terms are negotiated per-contract.
