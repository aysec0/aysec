// how it's built

Every solve becomes a star, placed by category (sector) and difficulty (radius). Star size scales with points. Center medallion is your level tier. Concentric rings = certificates earned. Pulsing dashed halo = active streak. Constellation lines connect solves within each category. Deterministic from your data — the same solves always yield the same DNA.
