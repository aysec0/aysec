// expectations

House rules

1
Be kind. Everyone was a beginner once. Some people still are. They are not your enemy.
2
Don't post flags or full solutions for active CTF challenges. Hints & partial guidance are fine. Full solutions go in #writeups after the challenge retires.
3
Show your work when asking for help. "I tried X, expected Y, got Z" gets ten times the response of "is anyone good at this?"
4
No illegal targets, ever. Practice on the lab boxes, on the CTF, on your own systems, on bug-bounty programs in scope. Anything else gets you banned + reported.
5
No spam, no recruiting outside #jobs-board, no NFT/crypto talk, no political flame.
6
Use threads. Long discussions belong in threads, not in the main channel.
