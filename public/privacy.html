Privacy Policy

Short version: I collect the minimum I need to run the site, and I don't sell or share your data with third parties.

What I collect

  • Account info — username, email, hashed password.
  • Activity — which lessons you completed, which CTF flags you submitted, your CTF score and rank.
  • Newsletter email — if you subscribe.
  • Payment metadata — Stripe handles your card; I see only the order ID, amount, and your email.
  • Server logs — IP address and request URL for debugging and abuse prevention. Auto-deleted after 30 days.

What I don't collect

  • No third-party analytics (no GA, no Mixpanel, no Hotjar — none of it).
  • No tracking cookies. The only cookie is your auth session (HttpOnly, SameSite=lax).
  • No advertising profiles. There are no ads.
  • No data selling or sharing. Ever.

Third parties

Two services handle data on my behalf:

  • Stripe — for payments. Their privacy policy.
  • Hosting provider — runs the server. They don't read application data.

Your rights

  • Access — email me, I'll send your data within 14 days.
  • Deletion — email me, full deletion within 30 days.
  • Newsletter unsubscribe — every email has a one-click unsubscribe link.
