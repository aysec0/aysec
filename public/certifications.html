Certification prep.

Curated exam-aligned curricula. Each cert page maps the aysec courses and CTF challenges that prepare you for it, with my honest take on what the exam actually tests, what it doesn't, and how to study for it.

How these prep paths work

For each certification I support, you get:

  • An honest take on the exam. What it tests, what it doesn't, and whether it's worth the price.
  • A curated course sequence. Ordered, with notes on why each course matters for that specific exam.
  • Aligned CTF challenges. Practice problems matching the categories and difficulty of the exam.
  • Exam tips. The non-technical advice nobody tells you — pacing, when to walk away, the report.

What this isn't

A guarantee. Cert prep is your work. These paths organize the material — the studying is on you.

Also: I'm not affiliated with any cert issuer. Pricing reflects public exam fees at the time of writing and may change.
